CRITICAL: Regulatory Hurdles

San Antonio has strict rules for non-owner occupied rentals (Type 2). You must verify these before buying.

  • Type 2 Permit Required: You are a "Non-Owner Occupied" host ($450/yr).
  • Density Caps: Max 12.5% of units in a block/building can be Type 2 STRs.
  • Hotel Occupancy Tax (HOT): You must collect/remit 16.75% total tax.
  • HOA Restrictions: Many condos prohibit STRs entirely.
