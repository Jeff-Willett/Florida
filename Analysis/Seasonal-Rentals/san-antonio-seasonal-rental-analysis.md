## 3. Short-Term Rental Regulations (CRITICAL!)

### Two-Tier Permit System

San Antonio operates strict short-term rental regulations that significantly impact your investment strategy:

#### Type 1 STRs (Owner-Occupied)
**Definition:** Property where host lives full-time on-site
- **Permit Fee:** $300 for 3-year period (increased from $100 in 2024)
- **Density Restrictions:** None
- **Requirements:**
  - Owner must reside on property
  - Can rent rooms or separate dwelling units
  - Includes single-family homes, condos, accessory dwellings

**Your Scenario:** ‚ùå **Does NOT qualify** (you'd only be there 6 months/year, not full-time)

#### Type 2 STRs (Non-Owner-Occupied) ‚ö†Ô∏è THIS IS YOUR CATEGORY
**Definition:** Property where host does not reside full-time
- **Annual Permit Fee:** $450 (renewed every 3 years)
- **Strict Density Restrictions:**
  - Only **ONE Type 2 rental allowed** in multifamily buildings with <8 units
  - In larger buildings (8+ units): Type 2 rentals capped at **12.5% of total housing**
  - Residential blocks: Maximum 12.5% can be Type 2 STRs
- **Requirements:**
  - 24/7 local contact for emergency response
  - Working smoke/carbon monoxide detectors
  - Fire extinguishers
  - Properly marked evacuation routes
  - No parties or events allowed
  - Must comply with local zoning

**Critical Implication for Your Investment:**
- You CANNOT operate as Type 1 (not full-time resident)
- Type 2 has severe density caps (12.5% maximum)
- **Many condos/communities already AT capacity** for Type 2 permits
- **You MUST verify Type 2 permit availability before purchase**

### Tax Requirements (SUBSTANTIAL!)

**Hotel Occupancy Tax (HOT):** 16.75% total
- State HOT: 6%
- City HOT: 9% (collected by platforms as of March 2025)
- Bexar County HOT: 1.75% (owner responsible)

**Tax Collection:**
- Platforms (Airbnb/VRBO) collect and remit state + city HOT
- **You remain responsible** for Bexar County HOT (1.75%)
- Monthly reporting required to both jurisdictions
- Failure to comply = penalties

### Enforcement (Serious Consequences!)

**Penalties for Operating Without Permit:**
- Fines up to **$500 per offense**
- **Each day = separate violation** (could be $15,000/month in fines!)
- Civil enforcement with administrative hearings
- Permit revocation up to 3 years for violations
- Platforms required to remove listings without valid STR permit numbers

**2025 Enforcement Enhancements:**
- Stricter enforcement than previous years
- Mandatory meetings with Development Services for repeat violators
- Online permit verification database
- Platform cooperation required

**ACTION ITEMS:**
1. ‚ö†Ô∏è Verify Type 2 permit availability in target building BEFORE purchase
2. ‚ö†Ô∏è Confirm HOA allows short-term rentals (many prohibit!)
3. ‚ö†Ô∏è Budget for $450/year permit + 16.75% HOT + compliance costs
4. ‚ö†Ô∏è Ensure property management company handles permit compliance
