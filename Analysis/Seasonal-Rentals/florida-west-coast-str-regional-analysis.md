## Region 4: Tampa Bay Area (Clearwater / St. Pete / Bradenton)

### Clearwater

**Regulatory Environment: ‚ùå (AVOID - Highly Restrictive)**

**Key Regulations:**
- **Residential districts prohibit STR** (anything < 31 days)
- Only allowed in Tourist Districts or Business Districts
- Monthly rentals only in residential areas
- Business Tax Receipt required
- Cannot advertise for daily/weekly rentals in residential zones

**Verdict: NOT VIABLE FOR STR INVESTMENT**
Unless you purchase commercial/tourist-zoned property (much higher cost), Clearwater is off the table for traditional STR strategy.

---

### St. Petersburg

**Regulatory Environment: ‚ùå (AVOID - Severely Limited)**

**Key Regulations:**
- **Maximum 3 rentals per year** in residential zones (consecutive 365-day period)
- Only unlimited in hotel/motel zones
- Business Tax Certificate required
- DBPR licensing required
- Zoning heavily restricts STR activity

**Market Performance:**
- Not viable for true STR business model
- Occupancy/revenue data not relevant (can't operate full-time)

**Verdict: NOT VIABLE FOR STR INVESTMENT**
The 3-rental-per-year limit makes this a non-starter for serious STR income. You'd earn maybe $3K-6K/year maximum.
